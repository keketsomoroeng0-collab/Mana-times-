Pitch a story or say hello

We welcome ideas for features, interviews, and collaborative multimedia projects.

To pitch a story, send us an email with the subject line “Mana times pitch” and include:

  • A short summary of your idea (3–4 sentences).
  • Why this story matters to students right now.
  • Any multimedia elements you imagine (photos, video, audio).
